Extra-curriculars

Explore beyond Academics


Aside from our main education subjects, we also have extracurricular activities that students can engage with to relieve stress or to serve as their alternative learning. This extracurricular activities are highly encouraged to them especially to those who are willing to commit on their passion and people who are still finding where they excel or wanting to develop their potential outside the main academics. Creative Fine Arts have these things to help students discover more on them or even hidden skills that can pop up on sports and clubs. For competitions, our school provides the needed materials and guides for students as well as the payment for registration fees, etc. Below are the following extracurricular activities that students can join with and for more information click the arrow buttons.


Clubs and respective Competitions


Journalism Clubs

Journalism has always been an activity to almost all the schools because it aims to not only to help students write, but also to help mainstream medias spread valid information to be voice to others, and to fight misinformation. Creative Fine Arts has this too! We have two different clubs for campus journalism, Bahaghari for filipino language and The Central Scholar for English language. Experience doesn't matter because as long as you have commitment we will teach you how to write a good school paper. So what are you waiting for? Join now!

Campus Journalism

It is relevant for both learners and the school community. For students, it is important because it trains and helps aspiring writers express their opinions and views on school issues.


Dance with Rhythm

Unleash the Beat: Introducing Rhythmic Fusion! Get ready for an exhilarating night of electrifying moves and pulsating rhythms at our exclusive club event featuring the sensational dance troupe, Rhythmic Fusion! Dive into a world of dynamic choreography that seamlessly blends hip-hop, jazz, contemporary, and more, creating a dance experience like no other. Watch as this diverse group of talented dancers captivates the stage with their synchronized precision and infectious energy. Every performance tells a unique story.


Club Volleyball Nights

Dive into the thrill of the game at our Club Volleyball Nights! Join us for an exhilarating evening of spikes, sets, and serves as we transform our venue into a volleyball haven. Whether you're a seasoned player or a casual enthusiast, our Club Volleyball Nights are the perfect way to bump up the excitement! Grab your friends and hit the courts for some friendly matches. All skill levels welcome! Sharpen your skills with tips and guidance from coaches. Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of a community passionate. Keep an eye out for tournaments!


Club Shuttle Smash

Welcome to Club Shuttle Smash! Join us in the exhilarating world of racquets and shuttlecocks, where passion meets precision for an unmatched badminton experience. Our club boasts top-notch badminton courts equipped with professional-grade flooring, ensuring a seamless playing surface for your best shots. Forge new connections and indulge in the camaraderie of fellow badminton enthusiasts. Beyond the court is a hub of social activity. Join us for themed game nights, friendly tournaments, and celebrations that bring our badminton community together.


Basketball Club

Dribble into the excitement at our club, where the love for basketball takes center court! Join us for thrilling hoops action, camaraderie, and the adrenaline rush that comes with every slam dunk. Whether you're a seasoned baller or just looking to shoot some hoops for fun, our basketball club welcomes players of all skill levels. Get ready for fast-paced games and a slammin' good time on the court. Bring your A-game to the court and let the basketball fever take over. Join us where dribbles and swishes echo in the air, and the spirit of the game unites us all!


Chess Club

Checkmate your boredom and join the intellectual excitement at our Chess Club! Our club is the perfect place to sharpen your skills, make new friends, and engage in the timeless game of kings and queens. Join us for a checkered adventure where brains meet brilliance, and every game is a strategic masterpiece. Check in with our club where every move is a step towards victory!
